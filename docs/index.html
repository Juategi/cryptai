Privacy Policy for CryptAI

Last Updated: January 16, 2026

Introduction

CryptAI ("we", "our", or "the app") is a privacy-focused offline chatbot application. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information.

Our Privacy Commitment

CryptAI is designed with privacy as its core principle. The app operates 100% offline and does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data.

Data Collection

What We Do NOT Collect

  • We do NOT collect personal information
  • We do NOT collect usage analytics
  • We do NOT track your location
  • We do NOT access your contacts, photos, or other device data
  • We do NOT use advertising identifiers
  • We do NOT share any data with third parties

Data Stored Locally on Your Device

CryptAI stores the following data exclusively on your device:

  • Conversations: Your chat messages and conversation history
  • App Settings: Your preferences (theme, language settings)
  • Encryption Keys: Security keys for encrypting your data

This data never leaves your device. All information is encrypted using AES-256 encryption (SQLCipher) and stored locally.

Data Security

  • All conversations are encrypted with AES-256 encryption
  • Encryption keys are stored in your device's secure storage (Keychain on iOS, Keystore on Android)
  • No internet connection is required or used by the app
  • You have full control over your data at all times

Data Deletion

You can delete your data at any time by:

  • Deleting individual conversations within the app
  • Clearing app data through your device settings
  • Uninstalling the app (this removes all associated data)

Children's Privacy

CryptAI does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under 13 years of age. Since all data remains on the device and is never transmitted, the app is safe for users of all ages.

Third-Party Services

CryptAI does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics platforms, or advertising networks. The app functions entirely offline.

Permissions

CryptAI may request the following permissions:

  • Microphone (optional): Only used for voice input feature. Audio is processed locally and never transmitted.

No other permissions are required for the core functionality of the app.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy.
